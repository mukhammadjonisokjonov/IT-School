About Our School

On the initiative of the Head of our Republic, the project “One Million Programmers” is being implemented in Uzbekistan, organized by IT Park Uzbekistan. Furthermore, in order to implement this project simultaneously in the districts of the Namangan region and Namangan City, 12 IT-Centers under the name of “IT School” were launched. It is noteworthy that the centers have opened in the buildings of professional colleges in the region, which have been renovated and equipped with furniture, modern computers and office equipment.

IT School Graduates

From the “IT School” of Namangan region will graduate 10,800 young IT specialists per year, who will receive special certificates from IT Park. Students of “IT School” who have successfully completed programming courses will be able to work in the Namangan branch of IT Park as freelancers and employees of the organization's residents.
