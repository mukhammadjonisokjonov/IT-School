IT School Background

The Ministry for the Development of Information Technologies and Communications of the Republic of Uzbekistan signed a joint decree on the merger of 12 IT schools in Namangan with IT Park.

According to the joint decision, 12 IT schools were transferred to IT Park. It was decided that several highly qualified IT specialists and teachers of informatics would work in each IT school in the region. Training and education at IT School is based on the IT Park methodology developed in cooperation with the Government of India.

Objectives of Namangan IT School

At the opening of “IT School” in Namangan city, the Khokim of Namangan region, Sh. Abdurazzakov also told youngsters that there are no age restrictions in the field of information technology and that they after learning programming, can earn from $2,000 a month, either being in their region or working for the export of IT services.

The youth of the Namangan region will be able to study relevant professions in the IT sphere and become in-demand specialists not only in their region, but also throughout the country, and even in the global arena.

IT School accepts youth with strong determination to learn programming!
